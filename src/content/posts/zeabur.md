# 这是个啥？

它可以连接你的VPS（前提，CPU≥1c，RAM≥2G）。然后在你的机子上面跑服务，包括

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/8e1adb47ca56287faadf230dd0a0213937cd87fd.png)

# 正式开始

进入 [My Servers - Zeabur](https://zeabur.com/servers)

点击创建

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/133767d3ff1b34281298b830788c1b6daf46e231.png)

点击添加自己的服务器

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/4b3fd976e549fae8cf4d69e363d157d2a276854e.png)

阅读要求，继续

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/4a9a9105121db3a13b603fb343a5ca6db2a25fd5.png)

填写SSH连接信息，让Zeabur连接到你的服务器

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/d439eb5216c92cfa0dd99568fa78910f05250480.png)

之后会开始安装k3s等工具，我们将不再需要手动SSH登入服务器了，直接在Zeabur的网页上配置即可！

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/cd393200cbeda64e35250af94666042ca46d0638.png)

接下来我们可以尝试部署服务，Zeabur将服务部署到你自托管的服务器是不收费的

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/7d378785a9f22ba651e0ab2654014ac6be156d3c.png)

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/d5690332cc961e1cdaa077485f885cf358893c12.png)

请自由发挥！

![](https://i0.hdslb.com/bfs/openplatform/8e1adb47ca56287faadf230dd0a0213937cd87fd.png)
